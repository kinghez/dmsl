    Our Story

    Divine-Mak is a wholly owned Nigerian company, incorporated under the companies and Allied Matters Act of 1990, with RC No 962116; It was registered after 16 (sixteen) years of experience and intensive research in electrical gadget, building projects, shoe factory and procurement.

    Since its commencement of business, Divine-Mak has steadily improved service delivery and as well enhanced our operations through innovative inspirations. We are following the trends of the dynamics in the electronic and electrical service sector, while working continually to create values that satisfy our customers, through a customer oriented and target driven strategic corporate plan.

    We are in business partnership with key companies within our industries that gives us technical support to further boost our ability to give speedy services now and in the future. Our business relationship with you shall not only be official but we aim of giving your company a first class attention and a good product and service experience.
